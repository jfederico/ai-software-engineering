# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus feedback on ideas and artifacts
- Assume good intent
- Accept and apply feedback professionally

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation
- Publishing private information without permission
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct and may remove or edit contributions that violate it.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to: maintainers@local.invalid

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.
